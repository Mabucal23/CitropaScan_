About CitroPaScan

CitroPaScan is an innovative web-based system designed to monitor the health of citrus fruits and leaves. By harnessing the power of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), it provides accurate disease detection, real-time scanning, and predictive analytics to help farmers and agricultural experts protect their crops and maximize yields.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with intelligent tools that enable early disease detection and sustainable agricultural practices, ensuring better crop health and food security.

Our Vision

To become the leading smart agriculture platform that revolutionizes citrus farming through AI and technology-driven solutions.

Common Calamansi Diseases

Awareness of these conditions helps in early detection and management.

Citrus Canker

A bacterial disease causing raised lesions surrounded by yellow halos on the fruit. Infected fruits often drop early.

Black Spot

A fungal disease that causes hard, dark, sunken spots on the rind. It reduces fruit quality and shelf life.

Citrus Greening

Spread by citrus psyllid. Fruits are small, misshapen, remain green, and taste bitter. Highly destructive.

Scab

A fungal disease forming wart-like, corky lesions on the peel, making fruits unattractive and lowering marketability.

Anthracnose

Fungal infection creating dark, sunken spots on ripening fruit, often spreading in moist conditions and causing rot.

Melanose

Leads to small, reddish-brown raised spots on fruit rind that can merge, forming rough patches ("sandpaper" feel).
